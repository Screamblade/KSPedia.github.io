This site is dedicated to the people who follow and LOVE the game Kerbal Space Program (KSP).

I wanted to create this to support new and inexperienced players by providing information on specific parts of the game that you many have trouble with. I also wanted to stop people being confused with what needs to go where.


This site is not 100% reliable and any may not include information on a part that you may need, it only is intended to offer help to people who are new to the game. I do still promote that you go to the official KSP wikipedia for more detailed information, this is just a simple site for simple tips and help.
